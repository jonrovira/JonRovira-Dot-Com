Supporting the candidate pair I believed was best fit to lead Northwestern's student government, the Noah And Christina website presents and promotes their platform.

  • GitHub Repository

Background

The elections for student-government President and Vice President are notoriously compettive at Northwestern and the campaigns in 2015 were no different. Working alongisde Noah Star and Christina Kim's marketing committee, I collaborated with Nevil George to build the website that would present the pair's background, platform, endorsements, and promote other marketing material such as social media feeds.

Outreach Components

Two of the goals for the website were to enlist the support of students and actively engage them on campus. These two components illustrate ways in which we tried to achieve those goals.

Endorsements Widget

As student leaders around campus contributed their ideas and latched onto Noah and Christina's platform, we placed a heavy emphasis on recognizing their support and endorsements. On the website, an entire section of the site is devoted to these students' testimonials.

End Result

Throughout the course of the official campaign week, the website had thousands of hits. When the campaign team celebrated Noah and Christina's victory, Nevil and I were confident that we had materially contributed to that success with the site's development.
